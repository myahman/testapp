         How do I view Emergency Work for this Pick? View Emergency Schedule information here How do I use BidWeb to find work with specific days off?

    There are 3 ways to find work with specific days off:

    1. Scrolling:

      As in roster sheets, positions in BidWeb are organized by Days Off. The list of positions starts with SAT/SUN off, and continues through SUN/MON, MON/TUES, (etc.) as you scroll down.

    2. Filtering:

      • Click “Select Filter”
      • Click the filter you wish to use.
      • Click Apply. The work will now be filtered

    3. Sorting:

      Sorting lets you order the work you are viewing by a criteria.

      • Click the “X” next to Roster and Position.
      • Click Select Sort Criteria, and click the day you want to see at the top.
      • Click the Triangles on each day so that they face downward, and click Apply.

    See BidWeb Refresher Guide for more information.

    For a live demonstration, please see Module 4 of the BidWeb Training.

         How many preferences should I enter in BidWeb?

    You can view roster positions and enter preferences in Bidweb anytime after the pick starts. The Rank Among Remaining number will show you how many people ahead of you have not picked at any time during the pick.

    About 11 hours prior to your cut-off time, work for the prior group would have been distributed and you will be able to see the most up-to-date available choices. It is recommended that you finalize your preferences then.

    Operators and Motorpersons should enter as many preferences as their rank among remaining.

    Note: The rank among remaining will match the Rank in group during the 11 hours Finalization window.

    For more information, please see Module 4 of the BidWeb Training, or the BidWeb Refresher Guide.

         What does it mean when a roster position is "No Longer Available" in BidWeb?

    The words “No Longer Available” will appear if a position within your Preferred Work Section was already picked by a previous operator. You must choose another preference to replace it from the Available Work Section.

    Please note that this only occurs within the Preferred Work Section. The Available Work section only includes work that is available: roster positions will disappear as they are picked.
